My Story..

I am a junior software developer who loves Jazz Music, Rap Music, and the NBA. I also love 50 miles bicycle rides.

In my capacity as a DJ I have recorded dozens of mixtapes, as well as performed at many a venue. I also have programmed a lot of music using both software such as Ableton, and hardware such as an MPC 1000.

While working for Radio 1190's "Basementalism" I worked on getting local artists charted on the CMJ Top 40 Hip Hop Chart, with a lot of sucess.

For 2 years I ran a local music blog called The Colorado Rap Report, I did a lot of interviews, reviews, and other music jouranalism. I did this to unify and bring people togther within the Colorado Hip Hop Community.
